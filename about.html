About BestCare Physio

Delhi NCR's most trusted home physiotherapy service — dedicated to restoring lives through expert rehabilitation.

Our Story & Mission

BestCare Physio was founded with one clear mission: to make specialist physiotherapy accessible to every patient in the comfort of their own home. Based in Dilshad Garden, East Delhi, our certified physiotherapists serve families across Delhi NCR — including Shahdara, Anand Vihar, Vivek Vihar, Vasundhara, Kaushambi, and Indirapuram — bringing evidence-based, personalised care directly to those who need it most.

We specialise in neurological rehabilitation — stroke recovery, paralysis treatment, and spinal cord injury rehab. Our therapists are trained in the latest techniques including constraint-induced movement therapy, gait re-education, and task-specific training. Every patient receives a comprehensive assessment, a tailored programme, and measurable goals that evolve with their recovery.

Over more than 10 years, BestCare Physio has helped over 1,000 patients regain independence and quality of life. Whether you need stroke rehabilitation, paralysis physiotherapy, or a trusted home physiotherapist — we're here for you. Book today via WhatsApp or call us.

About Sahitya K. Tyagi

Sahitya K. Tyagi is the founder and senior physiotherapist at BestCare Physio, bringing over a decade of dedicated expertise in neurological rehabilitation to patients across Delhi NCR. Holding both a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) and a Master of Physiotherapy with specialisation in Neurological Rehabilitation (MPT), Sahitya has committed his career to restoring independence and quality of life for patients living with stroke, paralysis, and spinal cord injury.

His journey into neurological physiotherapy was inspired by witnessing the profound impact that timely, expert intervention has on recovery outcomes. Sahitya recognised that the majority of Delhi NCR patients recovering from serious neurological conditions faced a critical barrier: the inability to access quality physiotherapy due to mobility limitations and the challenges of travel. This insight drove him to establish BestCare Physio as a dedicated home physiotherapy service.

Sahitya is a certified Stroke Rehabilitation Specialist and an active member of the Indian Association of Physiotherapists (IAP). He stays at the forefront of developments in neurorehabilitation, regularly updating his clinical practice with the latest evidence-based techniques. His patient-centred approach — combining clinical precision with compassionate care — has earned him the trust of over 1,000 patients and their families throughout his career.

Under his leadership, BestCare Physio has built a team of equally dedicated physiotherapists, all trained in neurological and musculoskeletal rehabilitation, all committed to the same standard of excellence and the same philosophy: expert, personalised care delivered at your home.

Why Home Physiotherapy Works Better

For neurological and serious conditions, home-based therapy delivers superior outcomes

Comfort & Familiarity

Recovery is measurably faster in a comfortable, familiar home environment. Reduced anxiety, better sleep, and a sense of security all contribute to improved neurological outcomes. For stroke and paralysis patients, the stress of clinic travel can set recovery back — home therapy removes this barrier entirely.

Personalised, One-on-One Attention

Every home session is a dedicated, one-to-one interaction between patient and physiotherapist — no clinic distractions, no shared equipment, no waiting. This focused attention means each session is maximally productive, with treatment precisely calibrated to the patient's current condition and progress.

Real-World Functional Practice

Home therapy uniquely allows the physiotherapist to address the patient's actual living environment. Therapists can work on navigating the patient's own stairs, furniture layouts, bathroom safety, and daily routines — making rehabilitation directly relevant to real life and dramatically improving functional independence.

Family Involvement & Education

Recovery from stroke or paralysis extends far beyond the therapy session itself. Home visits give family members and caregivers direct, practical guidance — how to safely assist with transfers, positioning, exercises, and daily activities. An educated, empowered family dramatically accelerates the patient's recovery journey.
