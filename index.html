Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our home physiotherapy service

Home physiotherapy is a professional physiotherapy service delivered at the patient's home by a certified physiotherapist. It is ideal for patients recovering from stroke, paralysis, surgery, or injury who find it difficult to travel to a clinic.

Yes, BestCare Physio provides home visits across Delhi NCR including Dilshad Garden, Shahdara, Anand Vihar, Vivek Vihar, East Delhi, Vasundhara, Kaushambi, and Indirapuram.

We treat stroke, paralysis, spinal cord injuries, gait and balance disorders, back pain, knee pain, post-surgical rehabilitation, and neurological conditions.

You can book by calling +91 9868487867 or messaging us on WhatsApp. We will schedule a convenient time for a home visit.

The cost depends on the treatment required and session duration. We offer affordable packages for home physiotherapy in Delhi NCR. Contact us for a personalised quote.

Stroke rehabilitation duration varies by severity. Mild strokes may see significant improvement in weeks, while severe strokes may require months to years of therapy. Consistent physiotherapy significantly improves outcomes.
